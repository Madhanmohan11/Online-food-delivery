About Us

"The Vision of Hot Restaurants has been to provide quality food at affordable prices.

Pillars or Strength :

Since our inception, providing quality and hygienic food has been our top priority. Each day, we take all the precautions against COVID-19 and maintain sanitized environments in and around Hot restaurants. To achieve our goal, we hire the best of the workforce, many of whom have been trained extensively and who have served our beloved customers for decades. Our employees have been our core strength. We as an organization have made sure to go above and beyond for their well-being. All of our above ethos have helped us immensely in serving authentic and immensely traditional food in India and abroad to our beloved customers.

With food innovation and business expansion going hand in hand every day, we at Hot Restaurant are always exploring and re-inventing new ideas. At the same time, we make sure to remain faithful to our authenticity that has always made Sangeetha special and stand out from the rest. At our restaurants, we guarantee to always serve the most classic delicacies, provide great hospitality and overall an experience which is very warm and inviting to say the least.
